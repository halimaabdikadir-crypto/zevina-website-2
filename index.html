    Coping Strategies

    🫁

    Breathing Exercises

    Slow, deep breathing activates your parasympathetic nervous system, reducing anxiety in minutes. Practice 4-7-8 breathing for immediate calm.

    ⏸️

    Study Break Techniques

    Take 5-minute breaks every 25 minutes. Stretch, walk, or look at something green. The Pomodoro technique maximizes productivity.

    🧘

    Mindfulness

    Focus on the present moment. Even 2 minutes of mindful awareness can reset your stress levels and improve concentration.

    📝

    Journaling

    Write down worries to externalize them. It helps your brain process emotions and find clarity. Try gratitude journaling daily.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I deal with exam stress? ▼
    Prepare early, take breaks, use relaxation techniques like deep breathing, and remember that your wellbeing matters more than any single exam. Break study sessions into manageable chunks and practice self-care.
    How can I balance school and rest? ▼
    Schedule rest like you schedule study time. Use the Pomodoro technique, set boundaries with your time, prioritize sleep, and remember that rest is essential for productivity, not a reward.
    What should I do when I feel overwhelmed? ▼
    Stop and breathe. Reach out to a friend, use our breathing exercise, or post anonymously in the forum. Break tasks into smaller steps and focus on one thing at a time. You're not alone.
